About Us

The first YC meeting, held in Manchester in 2004, was a great success, and we hope to build on that success from year to year. In 2004 the YCG were able to accommodate everyone who wanted to give an oral presentation, resulting in a total of 16 talks right across the physical, chemical and biological spectrum!

The good foundation that was laid in 2004 was realised at the BCA Spring meeting at Lancaster in 2006, where the second YCG meeting was held. The meetings to date have been attended in large numbers with excellent feedback. This has prompted the current committee to try and push the group forward to be recognised by the BCA as a special interest group in its own right.

We believe that the chance for younger scientists to interact and exchange ideas, problems and solutions, using this excellent medium is a fundamental necessity in the advance of cr ystallography, in all forms, in the UK. Therefore we are aiming to hold YCG meetings every year, immediately preceding the BCA spring meetings, to allow this to proceed.

Hopefully we will be able to continue in accommodating everyone wishing to present material, offering a less intimidating environment for younger scientists early presentations.
