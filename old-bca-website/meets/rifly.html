Yes, it's your chance to see the best science presenter on television LIVE at the best science venue in the UK.

On Thursday 28th October 1999 Adam Hart-Davis and the Screenhouse team will be staging a unique live event at the Royal Institution of Great Britain in Albemarle Street. There will be three shows during the day (see below for times) the first aimed at younger science enthusiasts (age 7 up) with their families, the others for 12-plus hero-worshippers and adults. All three shows will be packed with exciting live experiments. You will be able to ask Adam questions and meet him in person afterwards.

What is in the shows?

11.30: Smoke Rings, Bubbles and Steam

Excitement, suspense and a hint of danger - a live show especially for children. Suit from age 7 up - adults welcome too!

3 + 7 pm: Adam's Heroes of British Science

Adam brings to life the ingenuity and brilliance of Britain's greatest scientific minds – over 12s and adults.
