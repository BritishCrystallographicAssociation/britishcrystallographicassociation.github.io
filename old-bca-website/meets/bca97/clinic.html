BCA Industrial Group's Clinic on Instrument Sensitivity

Venue: Leeds, BCA Spring Meeting 1997

Date and Times: 14.30 - 16.30 Wednesday 16 April 1997 9.30 - 13.00 Thursday 17 April 1997

Topics:

    (a) Acquisition of high quality powder data.
    (b) Qualitative phase identification
    (c) Semi-quantitative and quantitative phase analysis.
    (c) Results of the UK Round Robin exercise

Clinic Director: Dr Ron Jenkins, ICDD

Registration: 50 pounds (includes a free copy copy of Dr Jenkins' book 'An introduction to X-ray powder diffractometry'). The clinic is limited to 40 people and early application is advised. Please note also that you must first register for the conference if you wish to attend this clinic.
