Biological Structure Sessions at BCA '97

Provisional Program

This year's BCA Spring meeting will be held at the University of Leeds. The registration fee is 75GBP (40 GBP for students) before March 3rd. Further information about registration and submission of poster abstracts can be found on our WWW site under http://www.chem.leeds.ac.uk/conferences/BCA97. The meeting will start on Monday, 14.04.97, 1:30 pm, with a plenary lecture on "Area detectors in chemical and protein crystallography" (by J.R.Helliwell)

Presentations are subdivided into a plenary lecture and three sessions:
