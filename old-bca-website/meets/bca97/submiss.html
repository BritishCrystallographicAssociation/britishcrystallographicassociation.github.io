      ABSTRACT SUBMISSION by E-MAIL

E-MAIL ADDRESS

Send ALL messages to the following e-mail address: bca@chemcrys.cam.ac.uk
Please do not send messages to any individuals unless asked to do so.

ABSTRACTS

  1. E-mailed abstracts must consist of in-line ASCII characters. For 1997, we will not handle any other formats, hence we cannot handle graphic inserts. If these are rquired, please prepare a camera-ready copy.
  2. If your abstract requires superscripts, subscripts, italic and bold fonts or greek (or other) special characters, then please indicate small amendments as an addendum to the email. In complex cases, please post a `formatted' copy to the Abstracts Organizer. These amendments, and formatting of title, names, affiliations and any references will be performed in Cambridge.
  3. As an addendum to the message, please indicate:
    (a) the authors who will attend the BCA meeting
    (b) the session (BSG, CCG, IG, or PCG) for which it is intended
    (c) Name, Address, phone and Fax number of the author responsible for queries.
  4. You will receive an e-mail reply containing the abstract number, together with any queries about the text etc. Please answer the queries promptly.
  5. Absolute deadline for e-mail abstract submission is 1 March 1997.
