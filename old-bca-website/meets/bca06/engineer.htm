BCA Spring Meeting 2006

Lancaster University

Tuesday 4 April to Thursday 6 April 2006

Scientific Program

Parallel Session: Crystal Engineering (joint with BACG)

Thursday 6 April, 13:15 - 15:45

Chairs: Nick Blagden (University of Bradford) & Kevin Roberts (University of Leeds)

Detailed Programme

13:15 Christer Aakeröy (Kansas State University) - Constructing Co-crystals with Molecular Sense and Supramolecular Sensibility

13:55 Dario Braga (University of Bologna) - Making Crystals by Reacting and Transforming Crystals

14:35 Jonathan Steed (University of Durham) - Anion Binding Venus' Flytraps and Tweezers

15:15 Chris Gilmore (University of Glasgow) - Snap: A New Way of Analysing the Results of Cambridge Data Base Searches and a New Tool for Crystal Engineering and Structure Prediction

15:35 Chris Sumby (University of Leeds) - Crystal Engineering with Derivatives of the Molecular Host Cyclotriveratrylene

15:55 Joshua Mckinnon (University of Western Australia) - Extensions of Hirshfeld Surface-based Tools: Decomposing Fingerprint Plots

          Tea (16:15-16:30)
