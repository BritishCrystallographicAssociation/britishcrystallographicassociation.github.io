Scientific Program

DNA Recombination and Repair

Tuesday 26 March, 09:00 - 10:30 & 11:00 - 12.30

Organiser: Peter Moody

This session on "DNA Damage, Recombination and Repair" will feature talks by:

  • Titia Sixma (Netherlands Cancer Centre) - DNA Mismatch Repair

  • Dale Wigley ( ICRF, Clare Hall) - Relating Structure to Mechanism in DNA Helicases

  • Daniela Stock (MRC-LMB Cambridge) - Structure of Reverse Gyrase: DNA Gymnastics at High Temperatures

  • Tracey Barrett (Institute for Cancer Research) - The Crystal Structure of a Eukaryotic Resolvase

  • Malcolm White (St Andrews) - Archaeal DNA Binding and Repair Proteins
plus one or two talks selected from the submitted abstracts.
