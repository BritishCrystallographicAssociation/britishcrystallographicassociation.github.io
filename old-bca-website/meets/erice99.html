Erice 99

A CALL FOR APPLICATIONS to attend a

European Science Foundation Euroconference

Crystal Engineering: From Molecules and Crystals to Materials

28th International Crystallography School.

"Ettore Majorana" Centre, Erice, Sicily, Italy.

May 12th to 23rd, 1999.

Directors: Dario Braga (Bologna) and A. Guy Orpen (Bristol)

Crystal engineering is an emerging field at the intersection of supramolecular and materials chemistry. This interdisciplinary School will address the design, modelling, synthesis, characterization, and properties of crystalline solids.

Topics:

  • crystal-directed synthetic strategies for preparation of
    • coordination polymers and networks
    • hydrogen bonded frameworks
    • networks, clathrates, nano- and mesotubes
    • inclusion compounds
  • techniques for the characterization of solid supramolecular aggregates
  • non-covalent interactions between closed shellatoms
  • theoretical calculations of crystal structures and properties
  • biomineralization and crystal growth in biomimetic environments -
  • kinetics and thermodynamics of molecular crystals and inclusion compounds
  • crystal nucleation and growth
  • applications in
    • optoelectronics
    • second harmonic generation
    • conductivity and superconductivity
    • charge-transfer and magnetism
    • solid state reactivity
    • nano and meso-materials

Applications (IN ENGLISH) must not exceed one A4 page or 50 e-mail lines and should include:

  • a. family name, name, age, sex, citizenship;
  • b. address, phone, fax, internet/bitnet e-mail (if used);
  • c. academic position and scientific interests;
  • d. title or abstract of a poster contribution (if one is to be submitted)

Young researchers should add a letter of recommendation by a senior scientist and a list of no more than five publications (do not send reprints). Please specify if financial support is required and justify the amount requested. This material plus 5 (preferably self-adhesive) address labels should be (e-) mailed by November 30, 1998 to Paola Spadon, with a copy to Dario Braga (addresses below). Each application will be acknowledged. The results of the selection of candidates will be mailed before the end of December 1998.

The total fee, inclusive of board and lodging during the course, is $1,320 US. Financial support to applicants is conditional on the success of the several applications submitted by the organizing committee to international and national sources. Usually three times applications as there are places are received. Early applications are therefore recommended.
