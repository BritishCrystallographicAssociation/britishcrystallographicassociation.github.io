3 Day Workshop in Powder Diffractometry

The International Centre for Diffraction Data (ICDD) is pleased to announce the first in a series of three-day workshops on X-ray Powder Diffractometry, which will be held at St. Helens College, Merseyside, UK from December 6-8, 1999. The workshop is intended to train participants in all aspects of X-ray Powder Diffractometry, including "best practices" for acquiring data and phase identification using the Powder Diffraction File. Topics will include:

  • diffractometer set-up and calibration,
  • specimen preparation,
  • semi-quantitative phase analysis.

The cost of the workshop will be £350 pounds sterling. Space is limited, so registrations must be received by ICDD before November 1, 1999. For more information, visit our web page at www.icdd.com, or contact Terry Maguire at the address or phone number listed below.
