Stop Press: New Fellows of the Royal Society

We are very pleased that three of our members have been made Fellows of the Royal Society this year. They are Professor Eleanor Dodson, Research Fellow at York, Professor Richard Nelmes, Professor of Physical Crystallography at Edinburgh, and Professor Venkatraman Ramakrishnan, Senior Scientist, MRC Laboratory of Molecular Biology, Cambridge. Our heartiest congratulations to all three. Full citations may be found on the Royal Society Website: http://www.royalsoc.ac.uk/.
