    • 'Travel Guide to the Scientific Sites of the British Isles: A Guide to the People, Places and Landmarks of science' Chalres Tanford and Jacqueline Reynolds 1995 John Wiley ISBN 0 -471-95270-2 p334 £16.99
  • Specific Towns and Cities, alphabetic by name of place.
    • 'London city of science'. This is a map published by the British Library with support from COPUS. Avialable from the Science Reference and Information Service of the British Library. In April 1999 moving to the British Library, 96 Euston Road, LONDON NW1 2DB tel (0171) 412 - 7677
    • 'London Science' Dennis and Sylvia Rosen 1994 Prion ISBN 1-85375-140-5 p224 £14.99
    • 'London National Portrait Gallery' and The Royal Society with support from COPUS have prepared a free leaflet 'Science and the Portrait' for visitors to the Gallery, St. Martin's Place, London, WC2H 0HE tel: (0171)306 0055. It is a trail round the portraits of scientists in the Gallery including the famous portrait of Dorothy Hodgkin by Maggi Hambling, you either love it or you hate it, it is not a realistic representation, instead intended to convey the busy scientist engrossed in her work.
    • 'The Oxford Science Walk' Sophie Huxley ISBN 0-952- 2671-0 publisher 'Science Walk Publications' 35 Marston Street, Oxford OX4 1JU. £2
