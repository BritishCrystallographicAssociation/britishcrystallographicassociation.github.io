In situ Diffraction

These Web pages provide information about in situ diffraction - both for those who are already active in the field, and want to keep abreast of recent developments or post up their own information, and for those who want to get started. Click here for an explanation if you are new to the subject.

The information is limited to X-ray or neutron diffraction mainly in the UK at the moment; It may be extended to other methods, such as EXAFS, and worldwide information later. This page is still under construction, please tell us your news and what you would like to see on this page.
