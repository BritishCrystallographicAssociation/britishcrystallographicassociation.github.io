PUBLICATION PROCEDURES FOR 'CRYSTALLOGRAPHY NEWS'

Submitting items

CRYSTALLOGRAPHY NEWS is published quarterly (March, June, September and December) by the British Crystallographic Association. The deadline for an issue is the Friday at the end of the first week of the month before the publication date, click here for dates for this year.

It is possible to advertise in the paper version of the newsletter, click here for prices and policy which is set by the BCA Council.

Camera-ready copy typed on A4 paper with generous margins is welcome at any time. The pages are reduced to A5 for printing so very small print may be illegible. Click here for further instructions for authors ASCII, TeX, HTML, or Postscript files may also be sent electronically to BCA@ISISE.RL.AC.UK.

Items may include news about crystallographers (for example awards, honours, retirements etc), reports on past meetings of interest to crystallographers, notices of future meetings, historical reminiscences, letters to the editor, book, hardware, software reviews, small ads and advertisements for job vacancies in crystallography.

PLEASE DO NOT FOLD PAPER DOCUMENTS TO BE INCLUDED IN CRYSTALLOGRAPHY NEWS, AND PLEASE SUPPORT THEM WITH STIFF CARD BEFORE POSTING.

If you think that you are a paid up member of the BCA but you are not receiving your copy of Crystallography News, please contact:
