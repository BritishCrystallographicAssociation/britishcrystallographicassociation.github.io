CRYSTALLOGRAPHY NEWS No. 57 Jun 96


An increasing amount of 'Crystallography News' is now sent to me in electronic form, so that I can bring most of it to you on the World Wide Web. I do not usually bother to store the pictures because I get bored waiting for images to load across a busy network. Let me know if you want to see the pictures too, and I'll see what I can do for the next issue.
