Dear XRD colleague,

Glaxo Wellcome, Inc., in cooperation with the International Centre for Diffraction Data (ICDD), is planning on sponsoring an X-ray powder diffraction conference focused on topics of interest to the pharmaceutical community. The three-day conference would be held at a location at the East Coast of the US in spring or autumn 1999.

Proposed topics for the conference would include:

  • issues involved in the patenting of crystal forms of drug substances (e.g. proper presentation of PXRD data in patents),
  • Rietveld methods for quantification of pharmaceutical crystal forms and more traditional quantification methods,
  • indexing of powder patterns,
  • structure determination of pharmaceuticals from powder diffraction data.

If you have interest in attending, presenting a paper or poster, or would just like to stay informed about the conference plans, please contact one of the following individuals.

We would very much appreciate, if you would complete the enclosed form. However, a simple reply to this letter (by letter, E-mail, phone, or FAX) is sufficient.
