Council Business - from the Secretary


1998 ANNUAL GENERAL MEETING OF THE B.C.A

Notice is hereby given that the Annual General Meeting of the British Crystallographic Association will be held on

Tuesday 7th April 1998 at 5.00 p.m.

in the Physics Building, University of St Andrews.

At this meeting we shall elect a President and a Treasurer. Both Prof A M Glazer and Dr S J Maginn are eligible for re-election and are willing to stand. There is one vacancy for an Ordinary Member of Council. Dr C J Gilmore, having served for four years is not eligible for re-election. The Exhibition Co-ordinator (Prof P Barnes) is now an Ex officio Member of Council. Nominations in writing should be sent to the Secretary to arrive before
1st February, 1998. Nominations for any post require a proposer and seconder, and an indication or agreement in writing that the nominee has agreed to stand for the post. Nominee, proposer and seconder should all be current BCA members.

European Crystallographic Association ( ECA)

The Inaugural Meetings of the European Crystallographic Association Council were held in Lisbon, Portugal during ECM-17 in August 1997 and the ECC (European Crystallographic Committee ) disbanded. It's last act was to decide that the next European Crystallographic Meeting should be held in Nancy, France in 2000.
The following officers of the ECA were elected:
President: Carmello Giacovazzo (Italy), Vice-President :Joel Bernstein (Israel), Secretary: Paul Buerskens (The Netherlands) and Treasurer: Sybolt Harkema (The Netherlands) . Three Ordinary members of the Council were elected:
Frank Allen (UK), Maria Carrondo (Portugal) and Peter Paufler (Germany).

The Secretary is re-drafting the Statutes to include the discussed amendments prior to the incorporation of the ECA in The Netherlands. At the closing ceremony of ECM-17 the President welcomed the creation of the ECA and urged all European Crystallographers to support the new organisation and help define it aims.


Editorial - Any old BCA Newsletters?

John Robertson has kindly donated his copies of the 'Institute of Physics Crystallography Group Newsletter' to form the basis of an 'Editor's Archive' of past BCA Newsletters. His collection runs from No,1 May 1957 to No. 64 (Dec 1980) and is almost complete. I would be grateful for copies of the few missing ones, No.s 3 (Nov 57), 9 (Feb 59), 16 (Mar 62), 22 (Mar 64) and 58 (Jul 79). Please send them to the editor at the address on page 64.

I would also be grateful for any older copies of the current series of the BCA newsletter. The archive has copies starting with issue no, 37 June 1991, copies of earlier ones would be very welcome. While doing your Spring Cleaning in 1998, don't just throw out old newsletters, donate them to the BCA Editor's Archive.

Note added Jan 98- Thanks, and still a few missing
Many thanks to all those who responded to my plea. I will try to get an index to these past issues as soon as I can. I now have almost a complete set of the newer newsletters, the missing ones are from 1982/3 no.s 3,4,5 and from the earlier series of XRAG newsletters no.s 3(Nov 57) 9 (Feb59) 16 (Mar62) 22 (Mar 64) and 58 (Jul 79).

Kate Crennell
