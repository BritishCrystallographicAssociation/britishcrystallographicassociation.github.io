Three Letter Acronyms

Glossary of Three Letter Acronyms seen in 'Crystallography News'

Where possible, three (or more) letter acronyms (TLAs) should be avoided in articles submitted to 'Crystallography News'. THe following list of TLAs have appeared in newsletters published since 1995; if you happen to know the meaning of the ones left blank below, please send a definition to the BCA Webmaster.
  • ACA American Crystallographic Association
  • AEA Atomic Energy Authority
  • AERE Atomic Energy Research Establishment, Harwell (now AEA)
  • AGM Annual General Meeting
  • APS American Physical Society
  • BCA British Crystallographic Association, in other contexts may be the British Cement Association
  • BSG Biological Structues Group of the BCA
  • CCD Charge Coupled Detector
  • CCDC Cambridge Crystallographic Data Centre
  • CCG Chemical Crystallography Group of the BCA
  • CCLRC Council of the Central Laboratory of the Research Councils
  • CD Compact Disc? as in CD-ROM
  • CIF Crystallographic Information File
  • CLRC Central Laboratory of the Research Councils
  • CP/MAS Cross Polarization Magic Angle Spinning
  • CRG Collaborative Research Group, something funded by EPSRC
  • CSD Cambridge Structural Database marketed by CCDC
  • CUP Cambridge University Press
  • DESY Deutsches Elektron SYnchrotron, Hamburg, Germany
  • DL Daresbury laboratory
  • DOS disk operating system, that on which 'Windows' sits on a PC
  • DSC Differential Scanning Calorimetry
  • DTA
  • EBS Electron Back Scattered Diffraction
  • ECA European Crystallographic Association
  • ECC English China Clays
  • ED electron diffraction
  • EMBL European Molecular Biology Laboratory, main site in Heidelberg, outstation at DESY
  • EPSRC Engineering and Physical Sciences Research Council
  • EDX, EDXA electron-dispersive X-ray Analysis
  • ESF European Science Foundation
  • EXAFS Extended X-Ray Absorption Fine Structure
  • FRS Fellow of the Royal Society
  • FT Fourier Transform
  • FWHM Full width at Half Maximum
  • GMR Giant magnetic resonance may also be referred to as
    CMR Colossal magnetic resonance
  • HEFCE Higher Education Funding Council for England
  • HMX (1,3,5, 7-tetranitro-1,3,5,7-tetra-aza-cyclo-octane)
  • HRPD High Resolution Powder Diffractometer, an ISIS instrument
  • HSE UK Health and Safety Executive
  • HTK
  • HTML Hyper Text Markup Language (the format used on the WWW)
  • ICDD International Centre for Diffraction Data
  • ICI Imperial Chemical Industries
  • IG Industrial Group of the BCA
  • ILL Institut Laue Langevin, Grenoble
  • INEL a company that makes X-ray detectors and XRD instruments
  • IR infra red
  • IOP Institute of Physics
  • ISBN International standard book number
  • ISO International Standards organisation
  • ISP Internet Service Provider
  • IUCr International Union of crystallography
  • IUPAC International Union of Pure and Applied Chemistry
  • JAC Journal of Applied Crystallography
  • JREI Joint Research Equipment Initiative
  • JSR Journal of Synchrotron Radiation
  • KBE Knight Commander, Order of the British Empire
  • LAD Liquids and Amorphous Diffractometer at ISIS
  • MAD Multiple Amorphous Displacement
  • MCD magnetic circular dichroism
  • MRC medical research council
  • MRD (part of tradename in IG section Philips Xpert MRD
  • MTDSC Modulated Temperature Differential Scanning Calorimetry
  • NCUACS National Cataloguing Unit for the Archives of Contemporary Science
  • NDB Nucleic Acid Database (Rutgers)
  • NHM The Natural History Museum, London
  • NIST United States National Institute for standards
  • NMR Nuclear Magnetic Resonance
  • NSF National Science Foundation of the United States
  • ODF Orientation Distribution Function
  • OUP Oxford University Press
  • PC personal computer, usually running the Microsoft Windows System
  • PCG Physical Crystallography Group of the BCA
  • PDB Protein Data Bank - see also RCSB
  • PDF either Powder Diffraction File (ICDD)
    or Page Description Format (Adobe)
  • PR Public Relations
  • PSD Position Sensetive Detector
  • RAL Rutherford Appleton Laboratory
  • R&D Research and Development
  • RCSB Research Collaboratory for Structural Bioinformatics - a US organisation which now maintains the PDB
  • RDX (1,3,5-trinitro-1,3,5-triaza-cyclohexane)
  • RI Royal Instituion (of London)
  • RISC reduced instruction set
  • RSC Royal Society of Chemistry
  • SAXS Small Angle X-ray scattering
  • SECC Scottish Exhibition and Conference Centre, Glasgow
  • SEM scanning electron microscope
  • SERC Science and Engineering Research Council (now epsrc)
  • SRC Science research Council, former name of SERC
  • STM scanning tunneling microscope
  • SUC Scientific Unions Committee of The Royal Society
  • TATP (tri-acetone-tri-peroxide)
  • TDS thermal diffuse scattering ? not sure about this one
  • TEM transmission electron microscope
  • TGA An uncompressed graphics image format. Copes with many bytes per pixel, and multiple colour planes well.
  • URL universal resource locator (the adress of a file on the WWW)
  • UV ultra violet
  • WAXS Wide Angle X-ray scattering
  • WWW world wide web
  • XAFS X-Ray Absorption Fine Structure
  • XANES X-Ray Absorption Near Edge Structure
  • XD computer software for charge density studies
  • XRD X-ray diffraction (Note the spelling X-ray not xray or x-ray)
  • XRF X-ray fluorescence
  • XRPD X-ray powder diffraction
Please send in defiintions to the editor, who will be pleased to add any others to this list.
