Issues of Crystallography News in 2001

Crystallography News in 2001, issues 76, 77, 78, 79

We plan to make each of the 4 issues of this year available as PDF files, these should be readable by the free Adobe Acrobat reader commonly available on most PCs, but I think they are bitmap images and so are not searchable. A few articles are available separately, they are listed below the title for each issue. There were four issues:

  • No 76 March 2001, (PDF size 1950K 33 pages, the contents is on page 2)
    1. 2 book reviews "Geometric symmetry in patterns and tilings",   "Nanotechnology:mechanical and Electronic Systems on a Molecular Scale"
    2. Honours for Jack Dunitz and Richard Nelmes
    3. Obituary Arnold Beevers p17
  • No 77 June 2001, (PDF size 1535K 36 pages, the contents is on page 1)
    1. book review "Diffuse Neutron Scattering from Crystalline Materials"
    2. Obituary Clifford Shull p5
    3. Education in Crystallography p26 a report of a discussion meeting held at the Annual meeting in Reading.
    4. websites on quasicrystals, Diffraction standards, The atomic structure of crystals, The Online Macromolecular Museum p34
  • No 78 September 2001, (PDF size 3420K 36 pages, the contents is on page 7, the first 6 pages are colour ads)
    1. 2 book reviewspages 14,15 "The Basics of Crystallography and Diffraction" 2nd Edition "Nobel prize Women in Science"
    2. People in the news, Dame Julia Higgins, New FRS Andrew Leslie, George Sheldrick, Ian Langford retires p6
    3. Royal Society of Chemistry Landmark to commemmorate the work of Dorothy Hodgkin in Oxford
    4. Walter Halg Prize for Jane Brown p23
    5. Website for a Mineralogy Database Page 24
  • No 79 December 2001 (PDF size 576K 36 pages, the contents is on page 1)
    1. Half Term Mineral Detectives at the Museum p12
    2. Education in Crystallography p11
    3. Computing and Teaching Microsymposium at ECM 2001 p26
