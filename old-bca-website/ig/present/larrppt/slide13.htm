This graph of d(00l).l versus l for data sets 11 - 22 is on a much narrower scale than the previous slide and shows the scatter of the values at low angles. The data sets with the vertical lines (eg 21) indicate that peaks were not present above or below this cut off in the data. Some were because of high background, others because of low intensity and some because the scan range submitted excluded some data points.
