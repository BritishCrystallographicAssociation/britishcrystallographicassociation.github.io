British Crystallographic Association
Spring Meeting 12 - 14 April 2005

Instructions for delegates arriving at the venue.

A confirmation of your meeting registration has been sent by post. This page provides additional information to help you find your way to the accommodation, registration desk and lectures.

Instructions for finding Loughborough University

Where to go on site at Loughborough University.

All delegates should report to the gate house off Epinal Way on arrival. You will be given directions to the venue and if necessary a car park pass and the number of the car park to use.

Day Delegates including Monday's satellite workshops - Head straight for the James France building - building 47 in the central park area of the universities map - click on central park and then the enlarge button.
You will find the registration desk and all the lecture theatres in the James France Building. There is floor plan of the exhibition hall showing registration desk and lecture theatres.

Accommodation check in:

Most accommodation is in the Village Park area in either the Faraday (buildings 22) or Royce (buildings 24) - see map - click on Village Park and enlarge.
The nearest car park is No 7 and you should head to reception in Whitworth (building 23) to check in for your accommodation.

Monday Evening Dinner
Monday Evening Dinner, for delegates that have prebooked, will be served from 18:00 in the Evelyn Richards Restaurant - building 19 in the Village Park area. You will need a ticket (satellite meeting delegates) or your meeting confirmation.
Delegates are advised to take dinner at 6pm to avoid long queues in the restaurant which we share that evening with 650 delegates from another conference.
