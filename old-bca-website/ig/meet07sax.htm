Small Angle Scattering Special Interest Group

2 - 3 July 2007 ILL, Grenoble

Download (300 kb) MEETING POSTER.

Delegate joining instructions: All registered delegates have been sent a confirmation E-mail. Please take note of this important information for delegates a 25kb PDF file. Link to Site Map. Meeting is in room 214 - CIBB building

Final updated information E-mailed to delegates on 29th June. View copy.

Print friendly meeting programme - a 30kb PDF file. Abstract page.

Fees: Full £180, Concessions and Speakers £90. Exceptional value - includes 2 nights accommodation and food!

Organisers: Richard Morris, Dave Taylor and Jeremy Cockcroft - contacting an organiser.

The programme will start at 9am on the Monday morning with a tour of the ILL facilities and will finish for a 12:30 lunch on Tuesday to allow delegates to travel home that day. An optional tour of ESRF on may be arranged for Tuesday afternoon.

Delegate numbers are limited and the meeting fees will include Sunday (1st July) and Monday (2nd July) nights B&B accommodation in the facility Guest House, coach transport and dinner at a restaurant on Monday, lunches on Monday and Tuesday.

Registration is now closed. All delegates have been sent a confirmation by E-mail, contact Dave Taylor if you need further information. Successful application fees cannot be refunded under any circumstances.

Extra Nights: We have a few rooms reserved for Tuesday 3rd July. To book an extra night please enter the details under special requests on the registration form. You must pay for the extra night ( approx 30 Euros ) on arrival at the on-site guest house.

Bursaries: A limited number of sponsored bursaries are available for this meeting. The bursary will cover the meeting fee but NOT travel. Bursars will be expected to give a short oral contribution on their work or interest in small angle scattering at the meeting. To apply for a bursary please complete a registration form and write "Bursary application" across the top of the form.

Travel: Link to the ILL transport page.
Links for UK flights in order of proximity to the venue Grenoble or Lyon or Geneva. Note that many low cost flights to the local airports only operate in the ski season (try Stansted).
