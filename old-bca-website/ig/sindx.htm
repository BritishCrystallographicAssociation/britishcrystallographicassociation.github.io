The types of meeting are:

  • Autumn - one day meeting of general interest usually held in November
  • Course - the fundamentals of a topic are fully explained
  • Forum - a meeting with a varied format designed to bring people together and encourage discussion
  • SIG - Special Interest Group meeting (single subject area of wide and diverse interest to a sustainable group)
  • Spring - the IG content at BCA Spring meetings
  • Wkshop - a workshop where a topic is covered in depth
