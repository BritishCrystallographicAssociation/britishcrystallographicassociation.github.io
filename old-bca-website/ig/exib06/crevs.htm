Sponsorship (more information)

The special edition of Crystallography Reviews publishes highlight papers from the previous years BCA Spring Meeting. A free copy of the ~110 page issue is distributed to BCA members and provides a very useful reference volume for years to come. Sponsorship supports this distribution and is offered by the page at a cost of £60 per page subject to a minimum of two pages (£120). An acknowledgement similar to the one shown below is printed in Crystallography Reviews and offers you the opportunity of cost effective promotion to a wider audience than BCA membership.
