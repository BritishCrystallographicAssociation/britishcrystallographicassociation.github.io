Public Transport.

On the day of the meeting, a minibus will leave the nearest west coast main line railway station - Wigan North Western at 09:30am and return at the close of the meeting. Contact Mark Farnworth at least 3 weeks in advance if you want to use this free service.

The nearest railway stations are at Burscough only a short taxi ride to Briars Hall (the nearest hotel). A lift from the Brairs Hall to the venue on the day may be available, otherwise you will need to book a taxi (contact reception).
