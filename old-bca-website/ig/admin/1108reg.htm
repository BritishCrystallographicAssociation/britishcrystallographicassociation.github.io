Click first button below to send: you will get an email to confirm your registration with an attached Invoice for payment of your fees. The supervisor of students will be contacted to confirm their full time student status.

You MUST pay the invoice we send to you by E-mail within 7 days to complete your registration.
 Payment should be made to the BCA Industrial Group Treasurer at postal address given on the invoice we send to you by e-mail.
We accept payment by cheque in GB pounds drawn on a UK bank or BACS bank transfer payments.
Please include a copy of our invoice, as proof of registration, with your cheque.
If you pay by BACS you must post a copy of the BACS receipt from your bank and our invoice to complete your registration.

We can now accept credit cards through the BCA secure payment system (Visa Debit, Visa Credit, Mastercard, UK only Maestro, UK only Visa Electron).
A link to a credit card payment form is available on the invoice we will E-mail to you.

Your registration ONLY becomes valid on receipt of your meeting fees.
