Our next XRF meeting.
Our next meeting is at the British Geological Survey, Keyworth, Nottingham on the 10th May 2006. We are pleased to announce that this will be a joint meeting with the Atomic Spectroscopy Group, Analytical Division of the Royal Society of Chemistry. Please make the effort and try to attend this low cost meeting. Check out the latest programme details and the contents of the delegate folder!
