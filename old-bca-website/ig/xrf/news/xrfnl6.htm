Welcome to the sixth issue of the British Crystallographic Association (BCA) Industrial Group dedicated XRF Newsletter. There is a copy of this Newsletter on the web.
Our next meeting is on the 14th May at the British Geological Survey, Nottingham and is again a joint meeting with the RSC Atomic Spectroscopy Group. It runs back to back with an XRD and Minerals meeting on the 15th May at the same venue. View: XRD minerals programme here.

Final call for papers 14th May 2008 XRF meeting.
The programme for the joint meeting with the Atomic Spectroscopy Group, Analytical Division of the Royal Society of Chemistry on 14th May 2008 is taking shape. Speakers so far include: Nick Marsh, John Mansell, Alison Burke, John Boyle and Anita Radovnikovic. We still have room for a few more talks so use the contact us link below to offer a contribution.
