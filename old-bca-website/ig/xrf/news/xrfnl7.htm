Welcome to the seventh issue of the British Crystallographic Association (BCA) Industrial Group dedicated XRF Newsletter. There is a copy of this Newsletter on the web.
Our meeting on the 14th May at the British Geological Survey was a great success and we are now seeking contributions for our 3 day meeting in 2009. More details on both follow.

Call for papers 21st-23rd April 2009 BCA Spring meeting - XRF sessions.
A three day programme of XRF sessions to include "General Applications, New Developments in Instrumentation and TXRF, Environmental - including a plenary and joint XRF/XRD session, Trace Analysis, Method Validation and Portable Instruments".
Please consider offering a talk in one of these sessions, more details and who to contact can be found from the link below.
