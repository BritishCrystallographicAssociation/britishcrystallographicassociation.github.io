XRF News :
12-May-2006

Our first one day joint meeting with the RSC Atomic Spectroscopy Group was a great success. The 70 delegates enjoyed a mixed programme of "user" talks, a tour of the BGS XRF facilities and a delicious buffet lunch.
Reports from the meeting will be published on the web in due course.
