Dear XRF subscriber,

Welcome to the third issue of the British Crystallographic Association (BCA) Industrial Group dedicated XRF Newsletter. There is a copy of this Newsletter on the web.
Many thanks to those who attended the very successful meeting in May and the generous meeting sponsors. Please consider giving a talk at our April 2008 meeting, our continued success depends on you and your friends and colleagues getting involved in our meetings.

Our recent XRF meeting.
Our meeting at the British Geological Survey, Keyworth, Nottingham on the 10th May 2006 was a great success. This joint meeting with the Atomic Spectroscopy Group, Analytical Division of the Royal Society of Chemistry was attended by 70 delegates and 7 companies sponsored the meeting. We are planning another joint meeting for 2008! A detailed report on the meeting with photographs is available on the web.

Our Next meeting!
17-19th April 2007 - XRF at the BCA Spring Meeting, University of Kent, Canterbury. This 3 day meeting offers an ideal training opportunity. The diverse programme should offer something for all and combined with the dedicated commercial exhibition makes this an important XRF meeting that you should attend.
