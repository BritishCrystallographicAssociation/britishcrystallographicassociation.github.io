Report on Canterbury meeting!
April 2007 - XRF at the BCA Spring Meeting, University of Kent, Canterbury. This second 3 day meeting with commercial exhibition had a varied programme and fortunately the weather was kind for the trek between lectures and exhibition!
Report...

Call for papers 2008 XRF meeting.
We plan to build on the success of our May 2006 meeting with another joint meeting with the Atomic Spectroscopy Group, Analytical Division of the Royal Society of Chemistry on 14th May 2008. The model of short user talks went down well with delegates and even first time speakers were put at ease by the friendly atmosphere and excellent facilities. Now is the time to start thinking about what you could offer in a short presentation of your XRF work at the 2008 meeting. We are particularly keen to get talks specific to minerals to provide a link with the XRD minerals programme at the same venue on the next day. Please use the contact us link at the foot of this page to discuss a possible presentation. To give you some ideas, a detailed report on the 2006 meeting with photographs is available from the link below.
