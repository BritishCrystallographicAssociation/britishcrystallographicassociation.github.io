KKB Saclay, Instruments

List of LLB Neutron Scattering Facilities

  • POWDER DIFFRACTION
    • 3T2 2 axes (20 detectors) high resolution
    • G41 2 axes-multi-detector 800 cells
    • G42 2 axes-(7x10 detectors) high resolution
    • G61 2 axes multi-detector 400 cells
  • Materials
    • 6T1 4 circles textures
    • G52 2 axes for strain determination
  • SINGLE CRYSTALS DIFFRACTOMETER
    • 5C1 2 axes, lifting arm, polarized neutrons
    • 5C2 4-circles
    • 6T2 2 axes, lifting arm and 4 circles
  • DISORDERED SYSTEMS DIFFRACTOMETER
    • 7C2 2 axes multi detector (640 cells)
    • G44 2 axes (48 detectors)
  • THREE AXES
    • 1T1 3 axes, thermal neutrons
    • 2T1 3 axes, thermal neutrons
    • 4F1 3 axes, cold neutrons
    • 4F2 3 axes, cold neutrons
    • G43 3 axes, cold neutrons
  • QUASI-ELASTIC SPECTROMETER
    • G32 Spin echo
    • G62 Time of flight MIBEMOL
  • SMALL ANGLE NEUTRON SCATTERING
    • G12 Annular detector (30) PACE
    • G23 XY detector (128x128) PAXY
    • G54 XY detector (64x64) PAXE
    • G55 XY detector (128x128), polarized neutrons PAPOL
  • REFLECTOMETERS
    • G3.bis Time of flight - XY detector (128x128) EROS
    • G.2.2 2 axes - polarized neutrons
