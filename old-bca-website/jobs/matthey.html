XRD SPECIALIST

Johnson Matthey PLC is a world leader in catalyst technology. The Technology Centre, based at Sonning Common, undertakes research work for the Group.

A vacancy has arisen for an XRD Specialist in our analytical group to provide a primary support role on x-ray diffraction techniques.

Applications are invited from candidates with a first degree in Chemistry and at least two years experience in either a research or industrial environment. The successful candidate will show some experience in phase identification, lattice parameter fit routines, crystallite size measurements, thin layer measurements and hot-stage analysis. They will therefore need to be highly motivated and computer literate.

In addition to a competitive salary, we offer the usual benefits associated with a large international company.

Applications must be made in writing with full CV and current salary details to: Mrs Georgie Floyd, Personnel Officer, Johnson Matthey Technology Centre, Blounts Court, Sonning Common, Reading, RG4 9NH or
