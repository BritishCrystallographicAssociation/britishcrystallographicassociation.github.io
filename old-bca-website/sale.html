Powder Diffraction Equipment Sought for Donation

The Department of Chemistry, University of Nairobi, Kenya are trying to develop competence, and eventually establish teaching and research programmes in X-ray analysis. Financial resources are very limited. The CCDC recently donated and commissioned a system for geometric data collection from single crystals, comprising a sealed source generator and cameras. A powder diffractometer is also required.

Any suitable equipment in working order would be gratefully received as a donation towards this initiative. Funding for transportation is likely to be available.
For details please contact:
