    • David Blow has written a new book,
      Outline of Crystallography for Biologists pub by OUP in April 2002, price £25 (paperback).
      The publisher says the main text is non-mathematical making it accessible to all biologists. The mathematics is contained in spearate boxes to offer deeper insight to those who want it. I would be grateful for any opinions from those who have read the book.
      Details from the OUP website at:   http://www.oup.co.uk/isbn/0-19-851051-9/

    2. Science Writing Prize 2001
      Science Writing Prize 2001 is the 3rd year of this prestigious THES/OUP (Times Higher Educatioal Supplement and the Oxford University Press) prize for previously unpublished work about popular science. THES/OUP are looking for well-written pieces aimed at a wide audience based on your particular area of science.
      • 1st prize: £2000 + the Oxford English Dictionary on CD-rom publication in the Times Higher Education Supplement
      • 2nd prize: The Oxford English Dictionary in 20 volumes
      • 3rd prize: £200 of OUP books in popular science
      The writing must be your original, unpublished work, in English, and not exceed 3000 words.
      Closing date: 31 January 2002
      Winners will be announced during Science Week 2002

      Full conditions and entry forms from: Sonya Allen, THES/OUP Science Writing Prize, The THES, Admiral House, 66-68 East Smithfield, London E1W 1BX
