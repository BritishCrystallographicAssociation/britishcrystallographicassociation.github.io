Information about Dorothy Mary Crowfoot Hodgkin

Dorothy Hodgkin
born Cairo, Egypt May 12, 1910
died Oxfordshire, England July 30, 1994

Professor Dorothy Crowfoot Hodgkin
First Vice President of the BCA 1982 - 1983,
presenting the 1st Dorothy Hodgkin prize to the winner, Durward Cruikshank (left),
on the occasion of her 80th birthday, Bob Diamond then the BCA President (right).
  • 1964 Nobel prize winner
  • Former BCA Vice President
  • Royal Society institutes Dorothy Hodgkin Fellowships for young researchers in June 1995
  • Collected Works in 3 volumes published 1996
  • 1996 Commemorative Postage stamp women of acheivement series
  • Program for the Memorial Meeting July 1996 and report
  • Memories of
    • Memorial address Dorothy Hodgkin by Max Perutz
    • Dorothy Hodgkin by M.Vijayan
    • Remembering Dorothy Hodgkin by Anne Sayre
    • List of books about her
    • Book review Dorothy Hodgkin: a life by Georgina Ferry
  • BCA Dorothy Hodgkin Prize and Prize lecturers
  • Dorothy Hodgkin memorial lecture given annually during the Oxford International Festival for Women
  • Royal Society of Chemistry Landmark plaque erected in Oxford in May 2001 to commemmorate her scientific acheivements.
