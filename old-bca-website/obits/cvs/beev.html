Birthday Party arrangements 10 March 1998

We have finally fixed a time to celebrate Arnold Beever's 90th birthday - on the day itself, 27 May, 1998. It will be a rather simple celebration, basically a talk by Professor Malcolm Walkinshaw and a tea party in Edinburgh.
All are welcome, but it would be very helpful if those who were planning to come would contact me (gould@ed.ac.uk) for more details.
Bob Gould
