INFORMATION about David Blow Published in 'Crystallography News'

David Blow

Born Birmingham 27 June 1931 died Appledore, Devon 8 June 2004

Prof David Mervyn Blow   BCA President 1984 - 1988
  • Fellow of the Royal Society 1972
  • David Philips and David Blow awarded 1987 Wolf Foundation prize for chemistry
  • Presented the 1st Lonsdale lecture on 'Enzyme Engineering' in Belfast on August 27th 1987 at the meeting of the British Association.
  • Biological Structures Group prize renamed in his honour 1996
  • Author of Outline of Crystallography for Biologists reviewed in Sept 2002
  • Honorary membership of BCA 2003
