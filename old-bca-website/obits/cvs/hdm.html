INFORMATION about Helen Dick Megaw

Helen Megaw
June 1st 1907 - February 26th 2002

  • Honorary member of the BCA 1998
    Summary of her achievments listed in 'Crystallography News' 1999
    Distinguished scientist from the Cavendish Laboratory Cambridge, where she was a student of J D Bernal. Made the first structure determination of a ferroelectric material, barium titanate. Early determination of ice structure led to her having an island in the Antarctic named after her. Major contributor on the structures of felspars.

  • Congratulations on her 90th Birthday published in 'Crystallography News' June 1997

  • Awarded an Honorary Degree of D.Sc 2000 article in 'Crystallography News' December 2000

  • Obituary in 'The Times' 'Lives in Brief' p 40 March 15th 2002
  • Obituary in 'Crystallography News' June 2002
