Information about people associated with UK Crystallography

This page contains a list of people associated with British Crystallography, together with links to other BCA pages with further information about them. Last updated 29 May 2006.
It is still under construction, please help by telling me other names which you think should be in this list.

I plan to add all the links to this list, meanwhile there may be further information in the links to other files listed below:
  • News of others who were not themselves crystallographers but whose work contributed to crystallographic techniques, or who wrote on crystallographic topics.
