Databases outside the UK

  • ICDD International Centre for Diffraction Data

  • Materials Safety Data Sheets a set of links from NIST to mostly USA sources
  • David Barthelmy's 5000 pages of entries in his Mineralogy Database
  • Canadian databases, METALS and the NIST Crystal data files
  • Protein Data Bank in 1999, old site at BNL
