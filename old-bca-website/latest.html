31 Dec 2000

  • Details of how students should apply for Bursaries at the Spring meeting in Reading have been updated

  • more jobs on our jobs page

  • Congratulations to Professor Richard Nelmes, University of Edinburgh and Senior Visiting Fellow at CLRC, on the award of an OBE in the New Year Honours List for services to science.

  • Call for Proposals
    European Facility for High Pressure Research, Bayreuth, Germany is funded by the EU under the 'Access to Research Infrastructure Program until 2003 Visiting Scientists from EU countries (except Germany) may be funded for periods of up to 3 months, all travel and subsistence are paid. for details see their web site at http://www.bgi.uni-bayreuth.de/
  • Applications invited at the EU Geochemical Facility, Bristol, UK Visiting Scientists from EU countries (except the UK) may apply to use the facilities. for details see their web site at http://eugf.gly.bris.ac.uk
  • BCA web site directory structure is changed to move directories within the CNews area to combine the 4 issues for a year into one directory, and the CVS directory, which contains files of biographical information has been moved from below 'admin' to below 'obits'. You may find you need to update your bookmarks to these directories.
