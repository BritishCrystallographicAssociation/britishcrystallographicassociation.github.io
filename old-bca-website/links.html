Links to Internet sites, specialist search engines etc

This page gives links to other sites with search engines or specialised science information, not ncessarily related to crystallography, but they may have something of interest, for example buying crystallographic books. I have looked at all of them myself and found something interesting, but please note that The BCA is not endorsing any of their products.
Please send news of any others to the BCA WebMaster BCA@ISISE.RL.AC.UK)

  • Science Line operate a telephone enquiry service from 1 to 7pm weekdays tel: (0345) 600 444. They have a panel of experts who try to answer any question you may ask, but will not do your fomework for you!. If they do not know themselves, they will try to find an expert. Previous answers are on their web site, lok at those first.
  • Select Science an independent online consumer magazine for laboratory scientists. It also serves as a specialist portal for the applied chemistry and life science industries.

    The site enables manufacturers to detail products and allows scientific consumers to comment on their application experiences of laboratory equipment (There is editorial censorship and manufacturers have a right of reply). There are a host of additional services including the latest industry news, conference information, employment vacancies and links to other related sites.
