Notice & Agenda for the BCA Annual General Meeting 2005

Notice

Notice is hereby given that
the Annual General Meeting of the British Crystallographic Association
will be held at
16.30 on Wednesday 13 April 2005
in the University of Loughborough

At this meeting we will elect a new Treasurer

Agenda

  1. Approval of the Agenda

  2. Apologies for Absence

  3. Minutes of the last AGM [published in the March 2005 issue No.92 of Crystallography News]

  4. Secretary's Report to Council [published in the March 2005 issue No.92 of Crystallography News]

  5. Northern Networking's Report

  6. Report of the Treasurer to include Presentation of the Accounts for 2004 and the Examining Accountant's Report

  7. Acceptance of the Accounts

  8. Elections to Council

    • Treasurer

  9. Appointment of Examining Accountant for 2005

  10. Any other Business

Christine Cardin
(Hon. Secretary)
