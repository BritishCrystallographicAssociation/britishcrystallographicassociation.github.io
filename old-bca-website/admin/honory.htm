BCA Honorary Members

The concept of honorary membership for distingished crystallographers was first instituted in 1998, and incorporated into the BCA Statutes in 2000,

A list of current honorary members (with year nominated) is published occasionally in Crystallography News:

  • Paul Barnes (2005)
  • Jane Brown (2002)
  • Duward Cruickshank (2003)
  • Bill David (2001)
  • Bob Evans (1999)
  • Bruce Forsyth (1998)
  • Mike Glazer (2005)
  • Mike Hart (2003)
  • Judith Howard (2003)
  • Aaron Klug (1998)
  • Andrew Lang (2002)
  • George Sheldrick (2005)
  • John Walker (1999)
  • Terry Willis (1999)
  • Michael Woolfson (2002)

Previous honorary members include:

  • Arnold Beevers (1999) (D.2001)
  • David Blow (2003) (D.2004)
  • Bill Cochran (1999) (D.2003)
  • Ron Jenkins (1998) (D.2002)
  • Helen Megaw (1998) (D.2002)
  • Max Perutz (1998) (D.2002)

(More details on the deceased honorary members are to be found in the obituary section of the BCA web site.)

Further details on members were made available in Crystallography News of December 1999.
