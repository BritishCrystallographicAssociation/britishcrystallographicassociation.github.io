BCA Corporate Membership

The BCA values its close ties with commercial companies involved with crystallography. To enhance these contacts, the BCA offers Corporate Membership.
The package has been revised (2006) to widen the appeal of corporate membership, especially for "facilities".
Corporate Membership is available on an annual basis running from 1 January to 31 December and includes the following benefits:

  • Up to 10 free BCA memberships for your staff;

  • A 10% discount on exhibition stands on the annual BCA Spring Meeting;
    OR - A promotional poster at the annual BCA Spring Meeting;

  • Free insert in the annual BCA Spring Meeting delegate bag;

  • Two free full registration to the annual BCA Spring Meeting;

  • Ten complimentary copies of quarterly newsletter of the BCA Crystallography News;
  • Corporate Members will be listed in every issue of Crystallography News and on the BCA web site with company logo and hypertext links to your corporate web site (see current members).

The cost of this class of membership is £ 750.00 per annum (no change) and, with the package of benefits valued in excess of £750, represents excellent value.

To apply for Corporate Membership, or if you have any queries, please contact the BCA Administrative Office.
